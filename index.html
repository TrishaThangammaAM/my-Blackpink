BLACKPINK

Biography

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They debuted on August 8, 2016, and quickly rose to global fame, becoming one of the most influential K-pop groups worldwide. Known for their fierce performances, iconic fashion, and chart-topping tracks, BLACKPINK has established a global legacy.
